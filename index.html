The Midi Tonic

50ml Midi Apéritifs Classic Red
100ml Premium Tonic Water
Orange zest

How to:
Fill a tumbler glas with plenty of ice
Add 50ml of Classic Red
Top with double the amount of tonic water
Garnish with a zest of orange

Enjoy.
The Midi Spritz

60ml Midi Apéritifs Classic Red
90ml sparkling wine
Soda water

How to:
Fill your wine glass with plenty of ice
Add 60ml of Classic Red
Top with 90ml of your favorite sparkling wine
Finish with a splash of soda water
Garnish: Orange wedge

Enjoy.
The Midi Sour

50ml Midi Apéritifs Classic Red
15ml grapefruit juice
15ml lime juice
20ml simple syrup
1 egg white

How to:
Shake all ingredients vigorously
Add ice, shake some more!
Double strain over ice in a tumbler glass

Enjoy.
